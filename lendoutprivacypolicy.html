• LendOut Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 9, 2025

  • 1. Introduction

LendOut (“we”, “our”, or “us”) values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, store, use, and share information when you use our mobile application, LendOut (the “App”).

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not use the App.

We encourage you to read this document carefully to understand how your data is handled and how you can exercise your rights regarding your personal information.

  • 2. Information We Collect
    • 2.1 Personal Information

The App allows you to record and manage your lending and borrowing activities. The types of personal information we collect include:

  •         Names of individuals you lend to or borrow from.
  •         Transaction dates.
  •         Amounts lent or borrowed.
  •         Notes or descriptions you enter related to your transactions.

This information is provided voluntarily by you while using the App.

  • 2.2 Cloud Storage Information

To provide seamless access across devices, all your data is stored securely in our cloud infrastructure.

  •         Data is encrypted during transfer to protect it from interception.
  •         Data is encrypted at rest to prevent unauthorized access.
  •         We implement industry-standard security measures to safeguard your information.

Important: LendOut does not collect device identifiers, location data, or other device-specific information. Your privacy is maintained by limiting the data collected to only what is necessary to operate the App.

  • 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide for the following purposes:

  1. Core Functionality: To allow you to add, track, and manage lending and borrowing records.
  2. Data Synchronization: To store and retrieve your data across multiple devices via the cloud.
  3. App Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve App performance through aggregated and anonymous data.
  4. Notifications: To send optional messages about updates, new features, or maintenance.
  5. Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and legal obligations, if required.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

  • 4. Data Storage and Security
  •         Cloud-Based Storage: All user data is securely stored in cloud servers.
  •         Encryption: We use encryption protocols to protect your data both in transit and at rest.
  •         Access Controls: Only authorized personnel and systems have access to user data.
  •         User Responsibility: You are responsible for safeguarding your account credentials and maintaining the confidentiality of your login information.

We regularly review our security measures to protect against unauthorized access, data loss, or disclosure.

  • 5. Sharing and Disclosure of Information

We do not share personal data with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  •         Legal Obligations: When required to comply with laws, regulations, or valid legal requests.
  •         Business Transfers: In the event of a sale, merger, or acquisition, your information may be transferred as part of the business assets.

Third-party cloud service providers may process your data on our behalf solely for storage and operational purposes and are bound by confidentiality agreements.

  • 6. User Rights and Data Management

You have the right to:

  1. Access your personal data stored in the cloud.
  2. Request correction or deletion of your personal data.
  3. Withdraw consent for specific uses of your information.

Users can manage their data directly within the App or contact support to exercise these rights.

  • 7. Third-Party Services

The App may utilize third-party services such as analytics or advertising platforms (e.g., Google AdMob).

  •         These services collect anonymous information to improve the App and provide relevant ads.
  •         Users may opt out of personalized advertising via device settings.

No personal transaction data is shared with these third-party services.

  • 8. Children’s Privacy

LendOut is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

  •         We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
  •         If you believe a child has provided information to the App, please contact us immediately to have the data deleted.
    • 9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information in the cloud as long as your account exists or as needed to provide App services.

  •         Users can request deletion of their account and associated data.
  •         Certain data may be retained for legal or business purposes, such as compliance with regulatory obligations.
    • 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in the App, cloud storage services, or legal requirements.

  •         Updates will be posted in the App or on our website.
  •         The “Effective Date” at the top of this document will be updated.
  •         Continued use of the App after updates indicates acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
